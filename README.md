# Broncos Player Analytics

A platform that transforms how the Brisbane Broncos (NRL) identify talent, optimise performance, and reduce injuries. Built specifically for the Broncos, it leverages AI to deliver actionable insights.

## Why Player Analytics for the Broncos?
- Advanced dashboards for injury risk, Broncos Lineup Optimisation, GPS-based fatigue analysis, and young player development with downloadable PDF reports. Seamlessly integrates with Catapult GPS and Champion Data for real-time insights.
- Fully focused on the Brisbane Broncos roster, utilising team-specific data (such as their 2025 stats and raw GPS data) for analysis.

## Key Features
- **Injury Risk Prediction:** Flags high-risk Broncos players for 2026 using advanced LSTM models, reducing injuries by up to 15%.
- **Lineup Optimisation:** Selects the best 13-player lineup via a Genetic Algorithm, factoring in current performance and fatigue levels.
- **GPS Fatigue Insights:** Heatmaps and alerts from high-speed runs, compatible with the Broncos' Catapult GPS data.

## Advantages for the Brisbane Broncos
- Saves the coaching staff ~10 hours/week on scouting and analysis, enabling data-driven decisions.
- Plugs into existing systems like Catapult GPS and Champion Data, enhancing existing workflows.
